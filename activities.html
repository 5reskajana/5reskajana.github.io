Whenever I find the time on a sunny day, I like to go cycling. My choice is always either the countryside or by the lake. It's a great cardio exercise and a good adrenaline booster. A few hours on a bicycle sometimes feel like therapy.

I never miss a chance to go skiing in the winter. I don't care if it is a Macedonian ski-center or somewhere abroad. I have been skiing since the age of six and I don't plan on stopping at least until I'm sixty.

There is no friend as loyal as a book. I stand by that. Reading is a daily activity for me and I can't imagine a better way to spend a rainy day at home or a sunny day at the beach.
